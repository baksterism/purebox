Popular Search Engines

  • Bing - Microsoft's attempt at a search engine
  • Yahoo - no description needed
  • DuckDuckGo - Concerned about online privacy? DuckDuckGo prides themselves as being the search engine that does not track or personalize your searches and results. They even offer handy visual guides on Google tracking and filter bubbling.

Meta Search Engines

  • Dogpile - A metasearch engine that fetches results from Google, Yahoo!, Yandex, Bing, & other popular engines including those from audio and video content providers.
  • Yippy - Yippy is a Deep Web engine that searches other search engines for you. Powered by IBM Watson.
  • Webopedia - An encyclopedic resource dedicated to searching technology terminology and computer definitions.
